Gryphons is not an official CCA of Singapore Polytechnic (SP). We are, however, a Student Interest Group (SIG) officially recognised by SP School of Computing (SoC).

Sorry! Unfortunately, our club is only open to students of SoC. However, we welcome all students from SP to attend our events and even those outside of SP to participate in our CTFs!

We recruit new members around the start of every new academic year. If you're a student of SoC, you might receive a mass email about our recruitment in your school inbox!

To join Gryphons, you must be a Year 1 or Year 2 student of SoC. If you wish to become an EXCO of Gryphons, however, you must be a Diploma in Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics (DCDF) student.

A form will be available for you to indicate your interest. After which, you'll be invited for an interview where we'll get to know you better! There will also be a mini-CTF for all students of SoC hosted around the same time where we will be looking out for potential candidates!

Having prior cybersecurity experiences or knowledge isn't our make-or-break criteria in deciding whether you can join us. We are looking for students with a growth mindset, a burning passion for cybersecurity and the open-mindedness to learn new things. You should also be willing to commit your time and effort to the club.

Follow us on social media to stay updated on our events! Give us a follow on Instagram (@sp.gryphons) and join our Discord server!
